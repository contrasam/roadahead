🏆 How to Win

  • 🚦 Stop at red lights and wait for green.
  • ➕ At cross junctions (after 0.8 km), cross traffic flows on your red — jump it and you may get T-boned!
  • 🚸 Stop for pedestrians at zebra crossings.
  • 🚑 Siren behind you? Change lanes and give way.
  • 🏫 Under 25 km/h in school zones, under 50 at cameras.
  • 🏥 No honking in hospital silence zones — even at slow autos!
  • 🚂 Wait at railway gates. 🐄 Slow down for strays.
  • 📱 Phone rings while driving? Decline it.

Every good deed grows your 🔥 streak multiplier. Every challan costs ₹, license points — and your streak. Lose all 12 license points and you're suspended!
